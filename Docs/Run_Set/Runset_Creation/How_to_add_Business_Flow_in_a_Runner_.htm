How to add Business Flow in a Runner?

 1. Select the desired runner and click on the + button as shown below to add the Business Flow.

         

                

  2. The window displays all the Business Flow in the solution.

        Select the desired one and click on the Select button Or double click on the Business Flow.

       

 

                   

    3. All the Activites and Actions in the Business Flow gets added to the Runner.

        Click on the added Business Flow to view its Activities and Actions in the runner.
