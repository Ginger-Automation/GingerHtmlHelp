Email Action

1.  Introduction    
     Email Action is used for sending email. Attachments can also be used while sending the email.
     Email can be sent in two ways:-
      a)    Outlook
      b)    SMTP


2.    How to Configure Action


    2.1. Using Microsoft Outlook

         1)    Double click on the Business flow. In the Activity Actions, click on ‘Add New’ button
               
         2)    Go to the ‘General Actions’ tab and select the ‘Email Action’ then click on ‘Add Action’ button given in the below  window

                

         3)    Below screen should be displayed. Outlook option for Email Method is displayed by default.   

               

         4)    The email address is mandatory in ‘To’ field. You can enter the Subject, Body and Attachment as per your requirement. 
                Click on Ok button to add the Action.
                


         5)    The Run Action button will send the email to the ‘To’ and ‘CC’ recipient.


    2.2.    Using SMPT

        1)    Double click on the Business flow. In the Activity Actions, click on ‘Add New’ button
               


        2)    Go to the ‘General Actions’ tab and select the ‘Email Action’ then click on ‘Add Action’ button given in the below window
              

        3)    In the below screen select the ‘SMTP’ email method   

               
 
        4)    Enter the SMTP details. Select ‘SendEmail’ from the ‘Email Action’ dropdown. 
               Enter the mandatory fields ‘From’ and ‘To’ email addresses, ‘CC’ is optional
               Enter the Subject, body and attachments if any.
               Click on Ok button will add this action to the Activity Action.

               
 
        5)    Run Action will send the email to the ‘To’ and ‘CC’ recipients.
