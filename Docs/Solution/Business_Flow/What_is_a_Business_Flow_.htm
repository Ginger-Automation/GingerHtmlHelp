What is a Business Flow?

Business Flow is a set of Variables, Activities Groups, Activities and Activity Actions. 
All these items are created under the Business Flow.
A solution can have multiple Business Flows and each Business Flow can have multiple Target applications.

Business flow in Solution tab
  

 

Business Flows can be created using below ways:-

     1)    By right click on the Business Flows and clicking the + sign 

             


     2)    By duplicating an existing Business Flow. For this right click on an already created Business Flow

               

 

     3)    By Copy Pasting an existing Business Flow
