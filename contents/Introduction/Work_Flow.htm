Ginger Work Flow

  1. Click on ‘Create New Solution’ Tab as highlighted below ( + Sign) button from the drop down.

                           

              

           Enter the details in the screen as given below. Platform is the one on which your application runs.

           The ‘Create’ button will load the solution

                         

                                                                                                                                                             

  1. ‘Flow 1’ will be by default added to the ‘Business Flows’. 

                     

   

  1. Double click on the Flow 1  or click on the ‘Automate’ button in the right panel.

          The  'Group' and 'Activity1’ will be added by default under the ‘Activities’ section.

             

  1. The Actions you can add by clicking the + button & expanding the Actions Library section.

              

 

              Either double click on the Action Or Drag& Drop the Action Or click on the 'Add to Actions' button as shown below.

             

  1. After entering the details in the Actions, user can then either Run the Action or Run the Activity or Run the Flow to execute.
