Email Action

1.  Introduction    
     Email Action is used for sending email. Attachments can also be used while sending the email.
     Email can be sent in two ways:-
      a)    Outlook
      b)    SMTP


2.    How to Configure Action


    2.1. Using Microsoft Outlook

        1)   Double click on the Business flow under Buisness Flow tab.  Click on ‘Add New’ button and click on Actions Library.
               

         2)    Go to the ‘General Actions’ tab and select the ‘Email Action’ then click on Add Action button shown as below.

                   

         3)    Double click on the Action to open it in Edit mode. Below screen should be displayed. Go to Delivery Method tab.                                          SMTP for Email Method is selected by default.   

                    

         4)    The email address is mandatory in 'From', ‘To’ field displayed in the Message tab. You can enter the Subject, Body and Attachment as per your requirement. 
               
                 


         5)    The Run Action button will send the email to the ‘To’ and ‘CC’ recipient.
