What is Activities Groups?

Activities Groups is the area in which set of Activities can be grouped together in the Automate tab.

   1)    New Activities Group can be added as shown in the below screen, click on the + button.
            

     2)     Click on the 'New Activities Group' button

               

           

   3)  Enter the desired Name and click on the Ok button.

        


   3)    The group name is displayed. Click on the Next button to add Activities in it.

             


   4)    Add the New Activities from the Solution are displayed in a new window which can be added in the group. 

           Click on the Finish button to add the Activity Group and New  Activities.
