How to create Shared Repository?

1.  How to create Activities shared repository?
        a.    Double click on Business Flow in the Business Flows tab.
        b.    Go to Activities Section and click on the "..." button as shown below.             

             Click on the 'Add to Shared Repository' option to add selected activity to the repository.
              Note: Activities section is present in both Business Flows Tab and inside the Business Flow.
             
             
        c.    Once the Activity is added to the Shared Repository, an orange star icon is displayed near the Activity name.

                   

        d.    Click on the 'Add Actions' button in the Business Flow tab and expand the Shared Repository
                           

        e.    Select the Activities section and the Activity will be displayed here as shown below.

                From here it can be used in other Business Flows.

                 

          
2.    How to create Actions Shared Repository?

        a. Double click on the desired Business Flow from Business Flow tab.
        b. Select the desired Activity and the Action.

        c. Click on the desired Action. Click on the "..." button and  select "Add to Shared Repository" option.                   

               

         d. Another way to add it is, click on the "..." button at the top right of the Actions list.

              


        e.    After the action is added in the Shared Repository, a star icon is displayed in the Action.

               

 

        d.    When the Action is opened in Edit mode, the star icon is displayed under the Details tab

                 

        e.    Activity is added in the Actions section of the Shared Repository

              click on the 'Add Actions' button under Business Flow tab.             

                  

                Click on Shared Repository and go to Actions tab.

                                           
                               
3.    How to create Variables Repository?

        a.    Click on the desired Business Flow under Business Flows tab. 
        b.    Go to the Variables tab in the right pane.

               

 

        c.    Select the variable and click on the ".." button on it. Select the "Add to Shared Repository" option.

                 Note: Variables section is present in both Business Flows Tab and inside the Business Flow.

                  

 

         d.    Another way is to select the variable and click on the ".." button on the top . Select the "Add to Shared Repository" option.

                       
                         
        e.    After the action is added in the Shared Repository, a star icon is displayed on the variable.
                                

        f.    It gets added in the Variables section of the Shared Repository.

               It is visible under the Resources tab->Shared Repository
                                  

 

                     
4.    How to create Activities Groups Repository?

        a.    Double click on the Business Flow under Business Flows tab. 
        b.    Click on the "..." button near the desired Activity Group.
                  Note: Activities Groups section is present in both Business Flows Tab and inside the Business Flow.

                    

               
        c.    After the Activity Group is added in the Shared Repository, a star icon is displayed near the Activity Group name.
                                      

        d.    It gets added in the Activities Groups section of the Shared Repository.

              Click on the 'Add Actions' button under Business Flow tab.             

                           

                 Click on Shared Repository and go to Activities Groups tab.
